Hour 2

Market briefing scheduled at NYSE open. Stock alert monitor running every 30 minutes with RSI, MA crossovers, volume detection.
Hour 4
Blog intelligence running
10 feeds from OpenAI, Anthropic, HN, Lenny's Newsletter, Shopify, a16z — all wired up. Daily digest scheduled.

The Point

What this means for product managers

This isn't a tech demo. It's a shift in what PMs can do without asking engineering for help.

01
Prototyping is now instant
Your fake door tests can now be real doors. You can validate assumptions yourself before writing a single PRD. The gap between idea and evidence just collapsed.
02
Automation costs → zero
Scheduled reports, alert systems, batch messaging, data pipelines — these used to need a dedicated engineer. Now they're a conversation. What does your backlog look like now?
03
WhatsApp is an untapped surface
98% open rates. 2B+ users. Zero app install friction. The entire e-commerce customer journey — discovery, support, recovery, reorder — can live in a chat thread.
04
The new PM advantage
PMs who can vibe-code — build through conversation — have an asymmetric edge. Not because they replace engineers. Because they stop waiting for them to validate ideas.
05
Agents > dashboards
Nobody reads dashboards. Agents that ping you when something matters — and only then — are the new monitoring layer. Proactive over reactive.
06
Conversational UI is eating everything
We didn't build an app. We built a system that lives in WhatsApp. For many use cases — especially in emerging markets — that IS the product. No onboarding. No downloads.
